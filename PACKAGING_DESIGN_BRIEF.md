### Design Constants (Same for All SKUs)
- Logo placement: Top center
- Background style: Orchard/garden landscape (consistent)
- Typography: Same fonts across all products
- Trust badges position: Bottom third
- Regulatory info placement: Bottom

### Design Variables (Change Per SKU)
- Fruit illustration: Unique per product
- Color accent: Matches fruit (see color table above)
- Product name: Large, centered
- Subtitle/benefit: Small, below name
- Background tint: Subtle color shift matching fruit

## Visual Style Guidelines

### Illustration Style
- **Type**: Realistic watercolor/botanical style
- **NOT**: Cartoon, childish, or overly simplistic
- **Reference**: High-end organic food packaging, premium artisanal goods

### Background
- Soft-focus orchard scene with depth
- Warm, golden hour lighting feel
- Subtle paper/organic texture overlay

### Fruit Placement
- Hero positioning, slightly overlapping frame
- Natural, appetizing presentation
- Show fruit in its natural form (on branch, with leaves when applicable)

---

## Trust Badges (Required on All Labels)

Include these three badges on every product:

1. **100% Fruit** - Circular badge with fruit icons
2. **No Sugar, No Preservatives** - Clean badge with text
3. **Farm Fresh Goodness** - Badge with leaf/farm icon

## Print Specifications

### Label Sizes
| Jar Size | Label Dimensions | Notes |
|----------|-----------------|-------|
| 100g | 80mm x 50mm | Front label only |
| 200g | 100mm x 70mm | Front + back label |
| 500g | 120mm x 80mm | Wrap-around or front/back |

**Note**: Confirm exact dimensions with your jar supplier before finalizing.

### File Requirements
- **Format**: PDF (print-ready) or AI/PSD (editable)
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI minimum
- **Color Mode**: CMYK
- **Bleed**: 3mm on all sides
- **Safe Zone**: 5mm from edges
